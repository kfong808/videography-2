The Secret Life of Walter Mitty


The Secret Life of Walter Mitty is my favorite movie of all time. This movie follows the life of a man going through the motions of everyday life. In the film he goes across the world on an adventure he could never dream of. Ever since I watched this movie I have strived to live life to the fullest.
