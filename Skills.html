Skills

This is what I can do

Logistics

My skillset is built upon the experiences that I have had in the outdoors. With the hundreds of hours spent leading adventure tours, I have mastered the skill of logistical planning. This skill of logistics allows my participants and I to plan out every detail of an excursion. This makes it possible to make the necessary preparations for when things go right, and when things go wrong.

Ropes

Any alpine adventure, rock climbing, or canyoneering trip requires an expert knowledge of rope management. Knowing what knots to tie in different situations can be the difference between life and death. With this skill I can keep you safe and your co-workers happy.

Wilderness survival

As a certified Eagle Scout, I have a variety of outdoors skills under my belt. With these skills I can provide medical attention if needed, build a shelter in the case we get lost, and supply food for the duration of the excursion.
